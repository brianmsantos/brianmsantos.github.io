You came here to probably check out some of the work I've done.


That doesn't mean you can't get to know me a little better.


I'm a San Diego native mostly. I did move around as a child due to the fact of my family being in the military. This made it hard to keep the same friends growing up but I found different ways of keeping myself busy.

I was always a curious child with quite an imagination. I did the usual Lego building and drawing and was always curious on how things worked. I liked to take apart things and put them back together. When computers became more prominent I became more curious and fascinated with them and how the whole thing worked. I ended up getting my business degree but I am back full circle to computers.

My curiosity and always wanting to learn something new and satisfying brought me to learning web development. I am now able to combine the passion of building things and affinity for technology together in my career with web development.
